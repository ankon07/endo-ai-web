Trust layer

Human-in-the-loop by design

Endora AI follows the same guardrails described throughout the app: AI-assisted screening only, no diagnosis claims, and specialist confirmation for every case.

Clinical safety language used in the product

  • Results are not a diagnosis.
  • A negative scan does not rule out endometriosis.
  • All findings require specialist confirmation.
  • Reports cannot be finalized without human review.

Operational guardrails

  • Low-bandwidth compression during upload.
  • Completeness scoring before submission.
  • Case states for pending review, reviewed, and rescan.
  • Structured report generation after AI inference.

Trust message

Use AI to surface better signals, not to replace clinical judgment.
